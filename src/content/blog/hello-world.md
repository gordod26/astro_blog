Every blog starts somewhere. This is mine.

I've been meaning to start writing publicly for a while now — not because I think I have
anything uniquely profound to say, but because writing is how I think clearly. Putting
ideas into sentences forces me to actually understand them.

## What to expect

No particular focus, no editorial calendar. I'll write about:

- Software and how I think about building it
- Books I'm reading
- Things I'm figuring out as I go

If even one of those sounds interesting to you, stick around.

## Why Astro?

I built this site with [Astro](https://astro.build) because it ships zero JavaScript
by default, keeps content in plain Markdown files, and stays out of my way. The best
tool is usually the one you don't have to think about.
